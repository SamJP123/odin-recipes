PB & J

Description

A classic for all ages to enjoy, this easy sandwich recipe is as versatile as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly, any kind

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on other slice of bread
  3. Put bread with spreads together
  4. Serve
  5. Eat
