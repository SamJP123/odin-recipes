Bowl of Cereal

Description

While it may be simple, a bowl of cereal is one of the best ways to start one's day. If you've always wanted to learn how to put milk and cereal into a bowl, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • Milk or other liquid
  • Cereal
  • Uhh, bowl?

Steps

  1. Put cereal in bowl
  2. Put milk in bowl
  3. Serve
  4. Eat
